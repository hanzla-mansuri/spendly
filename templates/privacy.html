Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is needed to provide the expense tracking functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by date or category.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is permanent and processed within 30 days of your request.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers. No personal or financial data is shared with Google or any other third party as part of normal app usage.

We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via the details provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
